Chili Tots
Full Recipe
Chili tots under some meleted cheese
INGREDIENTS
  • 1lb ground turkey
  • 1 can (15oz) kidney beans
  • 1 can (8 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 pack of chili seasoning
  • 1 cup of chicken stock
  • 1 carrot (diced)
  • 1 medium onion (diced)
  • 2 tomatoes (diced)
  • 1/2 shot of honey
  • 1 package (32 ounces) frozen Tater Tots
HOE DO YOU MAKE THE CHILI?
  1. Heat olive in a large soup pot and cook onions until carmelized.
  2. Break up ground turkey and cook thoroughly.
  3. Add chili seasoning packet and stir until well combined.
  4. Add honey (to taste).
  5. Add carrots, tomatoes, tomato paste, chicken broth, beans. Stir.
  6. Simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Let the chili rest for 5-10 minutes before serving.
